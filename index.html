About Me

I’ve always been driven by the challenge of learning new technology and figuring out how to maximize efficiency with minimal resources. Whether in software, hardware, or real-world applications, I love finding smarter, more sustainable ways to solve problems.

My passion for gaming pulled me into the tech world, where I’ve explored everything from AI advancements to game development. Watching how AI is revolutionizing industries—from assisting with learning to optimizing energy use—has been incredible. It’s exciting to see how technology can not only improve lives but also reduce waste and make processes more sustainable.

I’ve always had a builder’s mindset, whether it was assembling LEGO sets as a kid or putting together high-performance machines today. Looking ahead, I hope to take that even further—exploring sustainable technology, working with electric vehicles, and pushing for innovations that make a real impact.

Future & Current Projects

AI Dungeon Master & Local LLM Integration

My latest project explores the combination of Local Large Language Models (LLMs) with Dungeons & Dragons-style gameplay. The goal is to create a fully AI-driven Game Master that allows users to carry an entire RPG world in their pocket. Each new campaign will feature dynamically generated stories, characters, and quests, offering a fresh experience every time.

The challenge is to seamlessly integrate real D&D mechanics—including turn-based combat, character progression, and dice rolls—while maintaining the adaptability of AI storytelling. I’m working on optimizing LLMs to run locally on mobile devices, making it possible to experience immersive RPG adventures without needing an internet connection.

🌟 Key Features in Development:

  • 🧠 AI-Powered Game Master – Generates unique stories & adapts dynamically.
  • 🎲 True D&D Mechanics – Dice rolls, character stats, and progression.
  • 📱 Offline Play – Running fully local, no cloud dependency.
  • 🗺️ Procedural Worlds – Randomized campaign settings & encounters.

This project is a blend of my love for **gaming, AI, and problem-solving**, and I’m excited to see how far I can push the boundaries of AI-driven storytelling. 🚀
