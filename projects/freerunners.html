About FreeRunners

FreeRunners is a **high-speed 3D platformer** designed for players who love the thrill of precision movement. Inspired by **parkour** mechanics and speedrunning culture, it challenges players to navigate dynamic environments as efficiently as possible.

  • 🏃 Speed-Based Gameplay – Focused on precision movement & fast reactions.
  • 🌍 Dynamic Environments – Multiple obstacle courses & levels.
  • 🎮 Multiplayer & Solo Modes – Compete against others or practice solo.

Challenges & Solutions

Issue: Implementing smooth **wall-running mechanics** without breaking the physics engine.

Solution: Created a custom collision system that dynamically detects surfaces and adjusts gravity for seamless transitions.

Issue: Keeping **multiplayer latency low** while syncing player movement accurately.

Solution: Optimized network packets to only send critical movement data and introduced client-side prediction to reduce lag.

Tools & Skills Used

  • 🛠 **Game Engine:** Unity with C#
  • 🖥 **Networking:** Photon PUN (Multiplayer)
  • 🎮 **Physics System:** Custom character controller
  • 🎨 **Level Design:** Procedural environment generation

Future Plans

Right now, FreeRunners is a **solid prototype**, but I want to refine the level design and add **leaderboards for global speedrun rankings.** Additionally, I’d like to experiment with **VR support** for an even more immersive experience.
