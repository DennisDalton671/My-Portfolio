About FreeRunners

FreeRunners is a fast-paced 3D platformer inspired by Fall Guys, developed as my final project in college with a team of two other developers. As **team lead**, my primary goal was to learn Unreal Engine 4 while implementing multiplayer functionality and a global leaderboard system to enhance replayability.

  • 🏃 Speed-Based Gameplay – Focused on precision movement & fast reactions.
  • 🌍 Dynamic Environments – Multiple obstacle courses & levels.
  • 🎮 Multiplayer & Solo Modes – Compete against others or practice solo.

Project Goals

  • ✅ Master Unreal Engine 4 – Learn the Blueprint system and physics engine.
  • ✅ Implement Online Multiplayer – Create a real-time competitive mode.
  • ✅ Global Leaderboard System – Allow players to compare times worldwide.

Challenges & Lessons Learned

  • 🔹 Networking Issues – Debugging desynchronization and optimizing server-client communication.
  • 🔹 Physics-Based Movement – Creating tight and fair movement controls for fast gameplay.
  • 🔹 Team Management – Led meetings, task delegation, and milestone tracking.
