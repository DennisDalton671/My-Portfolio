Challenges & Solutions

Issue: Implementing smooth **wall-running mechanics** without breaking the physics engine.

Solution: Created a custom collision system that dynamically detects surfaces and adjusts gravity for seamless transitions.

Issue: Keeping **multiplayer latency low** while syncing player movement accurately.

Solution: Optimized network packets to only send critical movement data and introduced client-side prediction to reduce lag.
