About MiniGames

MiniGames is a collection of small games, each designed to explore different game mechanics and programming techniques.

  • 🚀 **Space Rocks** – A physics-based asteroid avoidance game
  • 🐍 **Neon Serpent** – A modern take on the classic snake game
  • 🐦 **Wing Dash** – A fast-paced platforming challenge

Technologies Used

  • 🕹️ **Android Studio** – Development Environment
  • 🎨 **Jetpack Compose** – UI Design
  • 🧠 **Custom Game Logic** – Implementing physics & movement
